Digital Inspection Bureau

Final Design Challenge

CHALLENGE MODE
Briefing Tutorials Rubric We Are Learning To Challenge
MISSION BRIEF

Design an Inspection Game

Your task is to design and build a Papers, Please–style inspection game using MakeCode Arcade. Players must act as digital inspectors, reviewing social media stories and deciding whether to APPROVE or FLAG them.

Your game should reward careful thinking and penalise rushed or emotional decisions. The rules of your game must be clearly linked to the manipulation rubric.

REQUIREMENTS

Game Requirements

  1. Include at least 5 different stories for inspection.
  2. Use at least 3 rubric categories in your game logic.
  3. Allow players to approve or flag each story.
  4. Give clear feedback after each decision.
  5. End with a summary screen explaining how well the rules were used.
PLANNING

Design Before You Code

Before opening MakeCode, sketch your game on paper or in a notes app. Use the prompts below to guide your planning.

  • What does the inspector see when a story arrives?
  • How does the player read and understand the story?
  • Which rubric categories affect the outcome?
  • How does the game show a good or poor decision?
  • What happens if the player ignores important warning signs?
EVIDENCE

Designer’s Notes

Along with your game, prepare a short explanation that answers:

  • Which rubric categories did you choose and why?
  • How do those categories appear in your game mechanics?
  • What do you want players to learn from your game?
PLAYTEST

Peer Review

  • Swap games with another group.
  • Use the rubric to test their game.
  • Give one clear suggestion for improvement.
SUBMIT

Submission Checklist

  • MakeCode Arcade share link.
  • One screenshot of gameplay.
  • Short designer’s notes (text or document).
